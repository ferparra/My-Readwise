	- This is confusing AF but **REALLY** important.
	  
	  CCS CCU -> You capture the carbon as you cause its release ('point source capture', eg a power plant)
	  
	  Carbon removal -> You pluck that carbon from the air and put it somewhere else (underground, in trees/soils, in the sea) 
	  
	  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/FPoSrb5VsAYOMz-.png) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/KetanJ0/status/1511541612563771401))
	- WHY THIS IS IMPORTANT
	  
	  If you capture the carbon as you cause it, you make **no difference** to the air (meh)
	  
	  If you capture the carbon just straight from the air, you **have a negative impact** on the stock of carbon in the air (quite good if you store it underground forever) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/KetanJ0/status/1511542218196086785))
