- Highlights first synced by [[Readwise]] [[Feb 11th, 2023]]
	- [Quantocracy](https://quantocracy.com/)
	  
	  Quantocracy is a curated mashup of algorithmic and quantitative trading blogs that aggregates some of the best articles available on the internet. They feature lots of thought-provoking and insightful articles in the quant trading domain. Quantocracy is followed by a huge community of traders and is considered one of the best in business. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gryyj3war1tfxk0v7dt9ryrr))
	- [Quantstart](https://www.quantstart.com/)
	  
	  Founded in 2012 by Michael Halls-Moore, QuantStart provides educational resources for potential and practising quantitative analysts. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gryyk4aw5tvvrkan7pj4cykd))
	- [Geeksforgeeks](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/)
	  
	  Started by a professor named Sandeep Jain at JIIT Noida, geeksforgeeks is a well-known computer science portal. Here you can find the answers to questions related to programming, algorithms, data structures and interview preparation material. The website is divided into different categories for delivering information and the blogs serve the purpose well. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gryykhcnfn5v4ba6jmynbrdg))
	- [Quantra](https://quantra.quantinsti.com/courses)
	  
	  Quantra is an e-learning portal that specialises in Algorithmic & Quantitative Trading. Quantra offers self-paced courses that are a mix of videos, audios, presentations, multiple choice questions, and highly interactive exercises. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gryykz6at5as86aynytwtfvt))
	- [Investments](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/sloan-school-of-management/15-433-investments-spring-2003/) - This course will help you to learn how to make good investment-related decisions by acquiring in-depth knowledge of analytical thinking, mathematical derivation, and financial markets. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gryym8042sj1xjw4f65vts8g))
		- **Tags**: #[[mit]] #[[courses]]
	- [Topics in Mathematics with Applications in Finance](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/mathematics/18-s096-topics-in-mathematics-with-applications-in-finance-fall-2013/) - This one helps undergraduate and graduate students with the mathematical concepts and techniques used in the financial industry. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gryyma08hftqjh0g0ktrqy8d))
		- **Tags**: #[[mit]] #[[math]] #[[finance]] #[[courses]]
	- [Trading Algorithms](https://www.coursera.org/learn/trading-algorithm#pricing) - The course is designed for investors and money managers who want to acquire the skills required to design their own trading strategies. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gryyndymznvvxzfx4nz56ast))
		- **Tags**: #[[coursera]] #[[courses]]
	- [Advanced Trading Algorithms](https://www.coursera.org/learn/advanced-trading-algorithms) - This course will provide you with eight ready-made trading strategies that are based on rigorous academic research. This course will also help you design your own trading strategy, backtest it and measure the performance. The learner will also be taught scientific ways of backtesting without succumbing to either look ahead or survival bias. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gryynqahv1ereaa0q6t7d3zz))
		- **Tags**: #[[courses]] #[[coursera]]
	- [Trading Strategies in Emerging Markets Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/trading-strategy) - In case you are an investor or a money manager and are looking to develop skills that can help you develop and test your own trading strategies in emerging markets then this is the course for you. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01gryynyzb3pb58cxs1yst7da3))
