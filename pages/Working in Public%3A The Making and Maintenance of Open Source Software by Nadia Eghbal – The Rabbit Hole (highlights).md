- Highlights first synced by [[Readwise]] [[Nov 19th, 2022]]
	- platforms add value to creators by first improving their distribution, exposing them to potentially millions of people
	- Maintainers – responsible for the future of a project’s repositories, whose decisions affect the project laterally – “trustees” of the code
	- Contributors – make contributions to a project’s repositories, ranging from casual to significant, but who aren’t responsible for its overall success
	- Refactoring is the process by which developers pay down technical debt: rewriting, simplifying, and otherwise cleaning up the codebase without changing its functionality.
